Welcome to Paws-a-Palooza!

Join us for a spectacular celebration of all things dog-related. Get ready for a paw-some experience filled with music, art, and surreal dog activities that will leave you howling with joy!

Live Music Acts

Prepare to be blown away by the incredible dog-themed bands:

Barkstreet Boys

These canine crooners will serenade you with their catchy tunes, delivering a paw-some performance that will have you singing along.

The Rolling Bones

Prepare for a wild ride with this energetic dog band, as they rock the stage with their infectious beats and rollicking melodies.

Woof Fighters

Get ready to jump and bark along to the high-octane sounds of this dynamic dog-themed rock band, bringing the house down with their powerful performances.

Paws Against the Machine

Join this rebellious pack as they unleash their unique blend of punk and doggie spirit, creating an electrifying atmosphere that will leave you howling for more.

Electronic Acts

Experience the cutting-edge sounds of the dog-themed electronic acts:

Techno Terriers

Let the pulsating beats of these four-legged DJs take you on a wild electronic journey, combining catchy melodies with infectious rhythm.

Rave Retrievers

Prepare to dance the night away with the high-energy electronic tunes spun by these energetic and enthusiastic doggy DJs.

Cyber Collies

Immerse yourself in a futuristic sonic landscape as these tech-savvy canines unleash their glitchy and mind-bending electronic compositions.

Electric Pugs

Let the electrifying performances of these charismatic pugs energize the atmosphere, fusing catchy melodies and infectious beats.
